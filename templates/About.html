"Our vision is to create a sustainable, affordable, and comfortable platform for inter-city travel. Our engineers and personnel have put in immense effort to ensure that our services are unparalled. One of the company's founding principle is "Be Customer Centric", thus allowing us to keep our goals aligned."
History
The company was founded by a team of five co-owners in the year 2022. What began as group project, has now become a registered rail company. The company began out with an initial selection of five major cities in Canada. The next 10-year plan is to increase this capacity to over 15 major cities, spread out in the country, whilst maintaining the level of services being provided to our valued customers.
Key Statistics
Numerous communities served through our various projects and services. High revenue growth forecasted for the year 2022. Numerous hiring opportunities have been created, with employee count in hundreds. The Company plans to go green by 2025. This will allow us to inculcate sustainable practices and use greener technology for our trains to reduce CO2 emmissions.
